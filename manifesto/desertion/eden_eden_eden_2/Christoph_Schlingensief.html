Photographic documentation of the ghostly apparition of Christoph Schlingensief’s Chance 2000, documentation of Aktion Baden am Wolfgangsee (Swim in the Wolfgangsee), 1998, on a billboard on Rruga SH54 – Surrel, Tirana, Albania, between 11AM – 12AM. Selected by La Société Spectrale. Courtesy of the artist, Bettina Blümner, and Aino Laberenz/Nachlass Schlingensief and all those who preserve Christoph Schlingensief’s work.
